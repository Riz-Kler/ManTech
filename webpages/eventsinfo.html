The "famous Oktoberfest" in September/October we celebrate in a tent with space for 2,000 people located in Albert Square. Entrance is free on Wednesday, £5 Thursday and Sunday and the busy Saturday there will be a £10 entrance fee and beer and food can be purchased. Alternatively, we offer a package with food, beer and seat reservation for only £34 on Wednesday, £39 Thursday and Friday and £44 Sat. that includes. Seat reservation (together) Snacks 2 x 1.5 pints of beer or 1 bottle of white/red wine (+£3)
